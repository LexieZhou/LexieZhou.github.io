Top Dog

Top Dog is a family owned and operated business that has been serving the best hot dogs in the area for over 30 years. We are located in the heart of downtown and are open 7 days a week. We offer a variety of hot dogs, cold dogs, and sides.

We also offer catering services for any event. We are also proud to offer mail order for those who can't make it to the store. We look forward to serving you!
